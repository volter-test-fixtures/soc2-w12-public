# HR / Personnel Security Policy

> **Owner:** [OWNER] · **Effective:** [DATE] · **Review:** annually (CC1).

## Purpose
Ensure personnel with access to [ORG] systems are vetted, trained, and de-provisioned properly — required by
SOC 2 even for very small teams.

## Policy
- **Onboarding.** Before access is granted: background check where lawful, signed acceptable-use
  acknowledgement (`acceptable-use-policy.md`), and role-appropriate least-privilege access
  (`access-control-policy.md`). Record the date.
- **Security awareness training.** All personnel complete security training at onboarding and at least
  annually. Record completion.
- **Offboarding.** On departure or role change, revoke all access (GitHub org, Cloudflare, provisioning/admin
  tokens, secret access) the **same day**; rotate any shared credentials they held. Record the action.
- **Roles & responsibilities** are documented; [OWNER] is accountable for personnel security.

## Evidence
Onboarding/offboarding checklists with dates, signed acknowledgements, training completion records, the
quarterly access-review records that confirm de-provisioning.
