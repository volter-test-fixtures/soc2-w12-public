# Risk Management Policy

> **Owner:** [OWNER] · **Effective:** [DATE] · **Review:** annually (CC3).

## Purpose
Identify, assess, treat, and monitor risks to the security, availability, confidentiality, and processing
integrity of this installation.

## Policy
- Maintain a risk register (`compliance/risk-register.md`); assess at least annually and after any incident
  or material architecture change.
- Score each risk Likelihood × Impact; assign an owner, a treatment (mitigate / accept / transfer / avoid),
  and a date. Treat high scores (≥ 12) with a tracked remediation plan.
- Map each mitigating control to the control matrix so coverage is auditable.
- Accepted risks require [OWNER] sign-off and an expiry/re-review date.

## Process
Identify → assess → treat → monitor → re-assess. Feed incident learnings (`incident-response-policy.md`) and
access-review findings (`access-control-policy.md`) back into the register.

## Evidence
Dated risk assessments, the register with owners/treatments, sign-offs on accepted risks.
